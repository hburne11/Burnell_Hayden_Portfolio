All About Me!

I have always had a passion for technology! I remember back in elementary school, filming and editing mini stick hockey videos with Windows Movie Maker! I am glad that I have the pleasure of going to school to dive deeper into computer applications!

My main focuses are Front-End Development as well as Graphic Design. I enjoy using multiple code editors such as Dreamweaver, Atom, Sublime, and Visual Studio. For the design aspect, I use different Applications in Adobe Suite, such as Illustrator, Photoshop, Premiere, After Effects, and XD.

When it comes to Web Design, I am a visual learner. I like to design the webpages first in XD or Illustrator before I even open the code editor.

I recently started up a RedBubble account where I post my latest designs! Click to check it out!

Thank you for checking out my Portfolio! Please feel free to download my resume off of my home page! If you have any questions at all or if you are interested in working with me, please fill out the form on the contact page and I will respond as soon as possible!
